The trap is sounding helpless. The fix is narrating your **process**, not your ignorance:

> "I didn't have prior experience with X, so I broke the problem down, looked at how similar systems solved it, evaluated two approaches based on [trade-off], implemented a prototype, and iterated based on what I learned testing it."  
> This shows curiosity + rigor, which is exactly what "Learn and Be Curious" (an actual Amazon leadership principle) is testing for.
